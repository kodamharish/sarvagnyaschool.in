About us

Director's Message

At Sarvagnya, we take immense pride in our curriculum, which focuses on unlocking the creative potential within each child. Our educational philosophy ensures that students not only excel academically but also develop the skills necessary to navigate a competitive, fast-evolving world. The foundation we lay here is designed to help them grow into confident individuals capable of making their mark in an increasingly complex and interconnected world.

Our core belief is that the quality of education imparted today will directly impact the future of our society. Sarvagnya is not just a school; it is an institution grounded in strong moral values and ethics, where students learn the importance of coexisting harmoniously with others. We strive to embed values that will shape them into responsible, empathetic individuals who will contribute positively to society.

As we look back for the past years, we take pride in the many milestones achieved and the numerous awards won by our students in academics, co-curricular activities, and sports. These accomplishments reflect the drive for excellence that runs deep within our institution. We remain committed to fostering an environment where the quest for knowledge is coupled with an unwavering dedication to personal growth.

As we celebrate the achievements of our alumni who have gone on to make significant contributions in various fields across the globe, we remain committed to the vision that has shaped Sarvagnya for over 5 years. Our school continues to evolve, and with the support of our dedicated faculty and staff, we are determined to elevate our standards even higher, creating a learning environment where every student can flourish and reach their fullest potential.

It is a privilege to be part of this journey, and I look forward to seeing our students continue to excel, innovate, and lead with integrity in the years to come.

Neelima Kollipara
Director

Our Vision

Our objective is to stimulate a versatile mindset in students, emphasizing scientific inquiry, aesthetic appreciation, and democratic values, thereby preparing them for thoughtful and responsible participation in society.

Our Mission

We encourage practices that urge coherent thinking, a respect for nature, the dignity of labor, fraternity, and the freedom of thought and expression, thereby rearing versed and responsible individuals.
